Welcome to the Super Number Sorter 3400!

Try to solve these number sorting puzzles, and climb the leaderboard!

How to Play

The aim is simple: take a scrambled 3x3 grid of numbers, and align them in order. Sounds simple enough, right? The catch is, you can only swap number locations with the empty spot on the grid.
For example, take this grid:

1
5
2
6
8
7
3
4

In this case, you could either slide 8 or 5 into the empty corner. For example, if you moved 5, the result would be this:

1
5
2
6
8
7
3
4

Your end goal, as described above, is to align the grid in ascending order. That would look like so:

1
2
3
4
5
6
7
8

Your final score on the leaderboard is determined by how many moves and how long it takes to sort the grid.
